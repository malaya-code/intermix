WHY?

WHY does Intermix matter? As a lifelong learner, the organizer believes that discussion can lead to profound learning.

While the world (rightly so) has countless book clubs, participatory and interactive media groups are far less common. Across disciplines, emerging research suggests that critical media analysis is a vital skill in the 21st century.

While researching these ideas and building the foundations of Intermix, the organizer created Annotated Bibliography so others could examine the rationale, theory, and precedents informing the group.
