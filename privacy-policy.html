Privacy Policy

  Intermix maintains a minimal, privacy-respecting approach to information collection and use. The site exists to support community discussion, educational activity, and event coordination.

Information Collected

  Information is collected only when voluntarily provided.   This may include:
  • A name or preferred name
  • An email address
  • Message content related to participation, facilitation, or questions
Messages are submitted through the site’s contact form. Contact form → Forms are handled using Contact Form 7. Submissions may be stored using Flamingo for organizational reference.

Use of Information

  Submitted information is used only for purposes related to Intermix, including:
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Coordinating sessions or facilitation
  • Managing communication related to events
  • Internal reference for educational or organizational continuity
Information is not sold, marketed, or shared for commercial use.

Data Retention

  Messages may be retained for reference. Records are reviewed periodically and removed when no longer needed.   Requests for deletion can be made by contacting the site administrator.

Analytics

  Limited, aggregated site-usage information may be collected to understand general traffic patterns and improve site function. Analytics, if used, are intended to be privacy-respecting. Tooling and configuration may change over time.   No attempt is made to identify individual visitors.

Cookies

  Cookies are not used for advertising or behavioral tracking. Any cookies present are related to basic site functionality or required third-party services.  

External Links

  Links to external sites may appear. Intermix is not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

Contact

  Questions about privacy or data handling can be directed to intermix[underscore]club@proton[dot]me.   Updates to this policy will be reflected on this page.
