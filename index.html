INTERMIX

Intermix, an interdisciplinary media discussion group, open and interactive to all.

Let’s keep it simple. Have you ever wanted a space to discuss a TV show or a piece of media through a specific philosophical lens? Or had a desire to unravel the intricacies of certain software? Intermix is that space.

WHAT?

Often, there are not spaces that aren’t book clubs, class, or therapy where adults can discuss multifaceted media.

HOW?

Anyone can submit a topic or piece of media they would like to discuss. Media must be accessible through open-access channels.

WHY?

Participatory groups outside of education are rare. Research shows community learning improves conceptual and technical skills. See the annotated bibliography.

WHEN?

Meetings begin monthly. To view sessions or sign up, see the events page.

CONNECT?

To stay updated across platforms, visit the Connect page.
