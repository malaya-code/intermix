WHAT?

So, WHAT’s this group about? Intermix is a monthly group that centers around one topic per month. Participants can delve into a specific form of media, be it a social media post, news article, poem, or chapter of a book; the sessions are meant to be adaptable, thought-provoking, and low/no cost. Hosted virtually on Big Blue Button, all are welcome to join regardless of origin, culture, or institutional status. Participants plan to explore a wide range of topics.

You can learn more WHY. Or even WHAT is Intermix, and HOW participants can sign up for a session.
